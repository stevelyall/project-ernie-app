SAMPLE CONSENT FORM: this is a demonstration version of the application and no data will be collected.


Dear Participant,


Thank you very much for participating in this study!

You have been asked Dr. Catherine Ortner of the Department of Psychology of Thompson Rivers University, telephone number 250 828 5497, to participate in a research project entitled ERA, which encompasses the following:


The purpose of the project is to assess how people regulate their emotions in every day life. You will be rating your feelings and use of emotion regulation strategies several times during the day. In addition, you will fill out some questionnaires assessing your values and well- being. Your answers will help us to find out in what ways people prefer to respond in different emotional situations.


This information is not looked at by individual answers. Instead, each person’s answers are combined with those of all other participants. You will be reading about some emotionally evocative situations of the type that could occur in everyday life, but there is no discomfort or distress anticipated from your participation in the study. Your answers will be kept completely anonymous and strictly confidential. All your answers will be analyzed anonymously on an aggregated level (i.e., over all answers from all participants taken together). We will at no point in time analyze your individual responses and responses will not be identified by the individual. Aggregate data may be presented at a conference or published in a journal.


The completed surveys will be stored in a locked filing cabinet in the researcher’s office for seven years, after which they will be shredded.


You may refuse to participate or withdraw your participation in this project at any time without any negative consequences. Your involvement or non-involvement in this project is in no way related to your status as a student. If you wish to withdraw, leave the questionnaires blank. If you withdraw, any data collected will be discarded.


You may ask any questions or register any complaint you might have about the project with either the chief researcher named above or with the Dean of Arts, TRU (250-828-5000).


If you have any questions or issues concerning this project that are not related to the specifics of the research, you may also contact the Chair of the Research Ethics Board – Human Participants (250-828-5000).


Copies of the results of this study, upon its completion, may be obtained by emailing the investigator, Dr. Catherine Ortner at cortner@tru.ca.


We appreciate your effort and help in completing this questionnaire. To begin, please continue. Your completion of the surveys constitutes your consent to participate in the study.


Thank you very much for your time and cooperation.
